# Contributing to Healthcare Reform Ideas

Thank you for your interest in improving healthcare! This document outlines how to contribute.

## How to Contribute

### Submitting Ideas

1. **Check existing ideas first** — Search issues and existing documents
2. **Open an issue** — Describe your reform idea with:
   - The problem it solves
   - The proposed solution
   - Evidence or examples supporting it
   - Potential challenges
3. **Submit a PR** — If you want to write it up yourself

### Writing Guidelines

- **Be specific** — Vague ideas are hard to evaluate
- **Cite sources** — Link to studies, news articles, real-world examples
- **Consider trade-offs** — No solution is perfect; acknowledge downsides
- **Stay constructive** — Focus on solutions, not just complaints
- **Bipartisan tone** — Frame ideas to appeal across political spectrum

### Idea Format

```markdown
## [Idea Name]

**Proposed by:** [Name/Source]

**The Problem:**
[Description]

**The Solution:**
[Description]

**Evidence:**
- [Study/article link]
- [Real-world example]

**Challenges:**
- [Political obstacle]
- [Implementation difficulty]

**Implementation Path:**
- [ ] Step 1
- [ ] Step 2
```

## Code of Conduct

- Be respectful and constructive
- No personal attacks on individuals or organizations
- Focus on policy, not politics
- Welcome diverse perspectives

## Questions?

Open an issue with the "question" label.
